PELOSI TRACKER

Nancy Pelosi’s disclosed stock trades

Under the STOCK Act (2012), members of Congress must disclose stock transactions within 45 days via a Periodic Transaction Report. This page will track Rep. Pelosi’s filings, group them by ticker, and compute the return on each position from the transaction date to today.
